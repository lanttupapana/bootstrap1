Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to visit Finland depends on what you're looking for. For winter activities and the Northern Lights, December to March is ideal. For summer adventures and the Midnight Sun, visit between June and August.

Citizens of the EU, USA, and many other countries do not require a visa for short stays (up to 90 days). Check the specific visa requirements for your country before planning your trip.

The Northern Lights, or Aurora Borealis, are best seen in northern Finland (Lapland) during winter (September to April). For the best viewing experience, stay in areas with low light pollution and monitor aurora activity forecasts.
